The filter will apply a low-pass filter to the emitted notes. This filter is defined in **notes**, i.e. `72` is the frequency of `c5` (523 Hz).

## Example 1

Play a chord filtered at 440 hz (A4).

<p class="shiny">C;4 i69</p>
